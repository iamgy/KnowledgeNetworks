The calculated effective dose rates varied between 2.8 and 25.4 mu Sv h(-1), with a mean value of 9.4 mu Sv h(-1), whereas the effective dose rates obtained for the in situ measurement varied between 6.0 and 28.0 mu Sv h(-1). The annual gonadal dose equivalent was calculated as 92.4 mSv. This is much higher than the world average dose equivalent rate to individuals from soil (0.30 mSv y(-1)).
