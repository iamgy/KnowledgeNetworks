Reliable control of Edge Localised Modes (ELMS) and disruptions is indispensable for the application of W. both to control the W transport in the edge and to avoid target melting in uncontrolled events which can seriously detonate the operational performance of the device. For DEMO and reactors, the behaviour of W under large neutron fluencies has to be further clarified and measures must be developed to mitigate degradation of material properties by neutron damage.
