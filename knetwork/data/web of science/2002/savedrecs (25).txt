In other solubility studies with amorphous Th(IV) hydroxide or hydrous oxide, considerably higher thorium concentrations are measured at pH 3.5-5. Therefore, solutions of comparable H+ and thorium concentrations are prepared by careful coulometric titration and examined by ultrafiltration, LIBD and X-ray absorption fine structure (XAFS) spectroscopy. These measurements demonstrate the presence of a large amount of small Th(IV) colloids. The Th L-3 edge EXAFS spectra of these colloidal suspensions are similar to that of the amorphous solid.
