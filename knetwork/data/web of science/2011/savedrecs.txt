The main objective of Cryotrap is to confine and reduce strongly the migration of radioactive volatile isotopes in beam lines. Mainly, Cryotrap is composed by a vacuum vessel and two copper thermal screens maintained separately at two temperatures T(1) = 80 K and T(2) = 20 K.
