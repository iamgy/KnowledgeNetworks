The oxygen partial pressure, pO(2), of the sintering gas in equilibrium with mixed oxide (MOX) pellets needs to be precisely controlled; otherwise it may induce a large dispersion in the critical parameters for fuel manufacturing (Gauche, 2013; Matzke, 1987).
