A comparison of sites is not a measure that could be allocated to the future users of the repository who are obliged to make contributions as a "necessary expense". Following a series of tests and checks for different parameters, a HWLS has been built and put into operation at the Tehran research reactor in 2009. Within this time-frame, it was realized that the HWLS could provide a better protection for reactor personnel against prevailing radiation under containment.
